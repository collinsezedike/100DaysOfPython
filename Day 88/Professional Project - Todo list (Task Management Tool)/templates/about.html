Task management tool to help you get more done

Feenee is a simple app that allows you to easily set and complete your daily tasks. You can write down all your tasks on the app and look down at a certain date to see what you accomplished. Task management should not be as difficult as the task themselves. Feenee adopts a simple interface so you only have to worry about accomplishing your set goals. You will no longer need complex logic controls for setting milestones - just use it with ease!

Feenee is from the french word, fini and is pronounced likewise.
